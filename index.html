G Flow

Stay in the flow

G Flow is a Chrome extension for managing unread Gmail messages and viewing Google Calendar events.

Its purpose is to allow you to stay on top of what's going on in your day, without needing to break away from what you actually want to be working on.

Things you can do with G Flow:

  • See a list of all unread inbox messages
  • View an email in full
  • Mark an email read or unread
  • Move email to trash
  • See today's and tomorrow's calendar events
  • Go directly to Gmail at the click of a button

G Flow does not store or share any data. Nothing leaves your browser. See our privacy policy.
